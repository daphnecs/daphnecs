Welcome to my research page! Here, I'll share insights and updates about my ongoing research projects.

My research focuses on rock moisture dynamics in the vadose zone (also known as the unsaturated or critical zone). I've primarily done work in the karstified terrain of the central Texas Hill Country, however I've also done work in various locations around California including Blue Oak Ranch Reserve, the field sites Povidence and Soaproot within the Sierra National Forest, and the Rancho Venada field site in the eastern foothills of the Northern California Coast Range.

Central Texas has seen 2 consecutive summers of exceptionally severe drought in 2022 and 2023. Through regular downhole geophysical monitoring of moisture dynamics in shallow boreholes at the White Family Outdoor Learning Center, coupled with surface water and meteoroloical data, I am exploring subsurface drought response in the deep root zone and the role these dynamics play in drought severity and recovery.
