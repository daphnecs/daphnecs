Welcome to my research page! Here, I'll share insights and updates about my ongoing research in Geosciences.

My research focuses on fractured bedrock deep root zone water storage in the vadose zone (also known as the unsaturated or critical zone), and the connection between vadose zone rock moisture dynamics to drought in Central Texas.

I am currently pursuing my M.S. in Geosciences at the University of Texas at Austin under the guidance of Dr. Daniella Rempe. If you're interested in the intersection of geosciences and environmental sustainability, feel free to explore my research updates!
